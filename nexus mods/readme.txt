## Fallout 4 Downgrader

### About
Fallout 4's recent next-gen update has broken compatibility with many beloved mods. Fallout 4 Downgrader allows you to easily revert your game to the previous version, restoring the ability to use mods like Fallout 4 Script Extender, Buffout, and more.

### Features
- **Automated Downgrading**: Just run the tool, follow simple prompts, and your game will be reverted.
- **Choice of Builds**: Choose between a smaller, framework-dependent build (requires .NET 8 runtime) or a self-contained build.

### Installation
1. **Download the Tool**: Choose your preferred version from Nexus Mods download section.
2. **Run FO4Down.exe**: Enter your Steam credentials when prompted and wait for the process to complete automatically.
3. **Enjoy Modding**: Restart your game and enjoy the freedom of modding again!
